Welcome to the BrandingPays eWorkbook!

The BrandingPays eWorkbook provides an electronic step-by-step guide through the BrandingPays five-step method for reinventing your personal brand.

If you want to get started right away

  • If you have not yet read the book (BrandingPays: The Five-Step System for Reinventing Your Personal Brand), we recommend that you proceed to the "Book" tab to step through the book's contents electronically.
  • If you have already read the book, then you might want to proceed directly to the "Workbook" tab to plan out your next big career move.

Introduction to the eWorkbook's user interface

User menu at top/right

At the top/right of the user interface, you'll see "Welcome, <your name>", followed by the small silhouette of a person, followed by a small down arrow. The small down arrow indicates a menu - in this case, the "user menu". On the menu, you can manage your account, your eWorkbook subscription and you can log out of your current session.

Top-level tabs

Note that there are four main tabs in the eWorkbook:

About
(This tab) Provides an overview of the eWorkbook application
Chapters
Step-by-step, interactive instruction that teaches you the BrandingPays five-step method.
Workbook

Your personal electronic workbook, where you can use the BrandingPays methodology to plan out your next big career move.

Note that, as you proceed through the interactive features within the chapters, your answers to the chapter questions will be automatically entered into your workbook.

Discussion
Quick access to any threaded discussions you might be having with colleagues about your respective workbooks.
Auto-saving to the cloud

As you step through the chapters and/or enter data into your workbook, the eWorkbook will auto-save your changes to a datastore in the cloud. This means the eWorkbook will remember where you left off, and if you switch to a different device (e.g., from your laptop to your phone), you can resume where you last left off.
