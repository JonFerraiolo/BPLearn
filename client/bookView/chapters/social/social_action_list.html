Chapter 8 Action List

◦Open social networking accounts, if you haven’t already, and make sure that your URL contains your real name.

◦Write a good profile that uses the work that you did in your positioning statement (chapter 2) and messages (chapter 3). Post your profile on the major social networking platforms.

◦Take a high-quality photo of your smiling self and use it consistently as your profile photo on your social networks.

◦Start with LinkedIn and add one or two other social networks, such as Twitter and Facebook.

◦Post content that is 90 percent about business and your field of expertise and 10 percent or less about business-friendly personal stuff.
