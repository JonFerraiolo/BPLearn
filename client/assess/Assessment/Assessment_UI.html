About the Personal Brand Assessment Questionnaire

The Personal Brand Assessment questionnaire allows you to ask colleagues for feedback on your personal brand.

There are four tabs:

  • About - (Current tab) Provides background information and instructions for how to set up a Personal Brand Assessment auestionnaire.
  • Self-Assessment - (Optional step) Allows you to fill out the assessment form yourself so that you can compare your self image with your colleagues' feedback.
  • Invitations - On this tab, you enter the names and emails of the people to whom you would like to send the questionnaire, and also enter a due date. When all entries have been provided, click "Send Invitations", which will send emails to your colleagues with instructions on how to provide feedback.
  • Results - After the due date arrives, you can click on the "Close Questionnaire" button, which appears on the Invitations and Results tabs. After closing the questionnaire, the software will consolidate (and partially randomize) the feedback, and you will be able to see the feedback by going to the Results tab.
